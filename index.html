What is Vida?

VIDA Coin is a cryptocurrency that rewards land owners for restoring and maintaining healthy ecosystems. Funded by companies and donors seeking real, measurable environmental impact, VIDA’s supply grows scarcer - and more valuable - as the planet heals. VIDA is issued through a transparent process that ensures every coin represents verifiable proof of impact.

About the Vida Coin Pilot

Turning verified ecological health into digital value

The Vida Coin Pilot is the first real-world test of the project, showing that caring for ecologically healthy land can generate real financial value via a digital currency (cryptocurrency) that runs on the blockchain.

Land → Value

Land owners register their land within the Vida system. The land receives a score based on its ecological health, and then begins to earn Vida Coins.

Monetary Rewards

Companies and individuals can donate to the Vida system. 100% of contributions go to purchasing Vida Coins from landowners.

The Pilot

The pilot is a collaborative test. The goal: to prove a simple, transparent system that can scale globally. Charities and Landowners are invited to help shape how this new model evolves.

This pilot lays the foundation for the fully automated version described in the white paper.

Transparency & Participation

Open verification

Every registered land parcel and reward is published openly, so anyone can see which areas have been approved and how Vida Coin directly supports real ecological restoration.

Each public record shows:

  • Visual proof and exact location of the verified site
  • A simple rating showing the land’s current ecological condition
  • The number of Vida Coins created and the blockchain record proving it
  • The group responsible for protecting or restoring the land (shared only with their consent)

The Road Ahead

Building the future of measurable restoration

The Vida Coin Pilot is a public-interest experiment, not an investment product. It’s the foundation for a transparent system where verified ecological recovery is rewarded directly through blockchain technology.

Next steps include:

  • Partnering with charities, environmental organisations, and eventually individual land owners
  • Automating land verification, ecological health calculations, and token issuance
  • Onboarding donations and corporate partners who fund restoration
  • Migrating pilot participants into the full Vida Coin network once live

Our goal is simple - to make ecological care measurable, verifiable, and directly rewarded.
