Adding VIDA to MetaMask (Desktop)

If you’re on desktop with the MetaMask extension, we’ll try to switch to Base and add the token automatically.

For Mobile follow the 10 simple steps below:

Step-by-step (mobile)

1

Install MetaMask from the App Store and tap "create a new wallet".

2

2. Select "use secret recovery phase" and setup your pass phrase.

3

Once inside MetaMask, tap the "popular networks" menu at the top and select Base.

4

Tap Receive and tap the small squares to copy your wallet address (it begins with 0x...).

5

Now send this address to us in your email along with your verification materials.

6

Tap the small + icon in the top right corner.

7

Select Custom token and make sure the network is Base.

8

Paste this address under “Token contract address”:

0x1D929E2422AEC567907f7fE2B966f7f84CA1858F

9

Tap Next → Import.

10

Congratulations you’ll now see VIDA Coin listed in your wallet under the Base network, it will show 0 until you have earn your first coins.
