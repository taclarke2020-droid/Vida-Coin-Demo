Set up your VIDA wallet

Follow the pictures below to install MetaMask on your phone, switch to the Base network, copy your wallet address, and add the VIDA token.

Step-by-step (mobile)

  1. Install MetaMask from the App Store and tap “Create a new wallet”.
  1. Select Use Secret Recovery Phrase. Keep your recovery phrase safe and private—never share it with anyone.
  1. Open MetaMask. Tap the network menu at the top and choose Base.
  1. Tap Receive (or your account name) and tap the copy icon to copy your wallet address (it begins with 0x...).
